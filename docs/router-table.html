Top & Fence

MATERIALS

Piece # Material Height Width
top 2 plywood, 19 mm1 600 mm 900 mm
fence-back 3 plywood, 19 mm 80 mm 750 mm
fence-bottom 1 plywood, 19 mm 150 mm 750 mm

  • 1 3/4”

    • STEP A | Prep

    1. Glue the fence-back x3 pieces together. Let dry.
    2. Glue the top x2 pieces together. Let dry.

    STEP B | Template

    1. Use the paper template provided with the router plate to make a wood template. Position the wood template centered on the prepared top.
    2. Trace the wood template opening onto the top and remove.

    STEP C | Inner Opening

    1. Drill a hole in the center of the top in the traced opening.
    2. Starting from the center hole, use a jigsaw to rough out the opening.2
    3. Replace the wood template and use a router with a template bit to clean up the opening
    4. Remove the template

  • 2 attempt to get as close as possible to the traced opening without touching

    • STEP D | Outter Opening

    1. Set the router depth to just past the thickness of the router plate.3
    2. Use a router with a rabbet bit to route out the pocket for the router plate.

  • 3 leveling pins will bring this flush

    • STEP E | Channel

    1. Route out the dovetail tracks in the top positioned 19 mm away from both edges of the router plate
